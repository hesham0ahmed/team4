Our mentoring program and how it works

For a lot of our recently graduated course members it is really important to have someone like a mentor for their first steps into the tech-job-world.
Starting work in an often totally new working sector comes with a lot of fear and anxiety.
Is there something better then sharing your experience with other young people?
The mentoring program won't just bring you new working and tech ideas but maybe also a new friend.
Connect through your interest in coding!
