About Us

Founded in 2016, CodeFactory Wien follows the concept of programming boot camps of Anglo-American countries. Our goal is to provide time-efficient and very practice-oriented training with the aim of the fastest possible career entry as a junior developer.

The Alumni Club is a foundation designed to unite participants and help them develop
in the technology industry.
